Situated approximately 35 km southwest of Nanaimo, BC, the Nanaimo Lakes Campsite is located on the shores of First Lake.

Thank you for your interest in our campsites. We look forward to seeing you this summer!

Facilities

Families and Seniors can enjoy 75 rustic campsites at three sites: Old Mill, Windy Point and Riverside   Old Mill Map
Windy Point Map

Group camping is available at Deadwood campsite.
Deadwood Map

  • Fire pits and picnic tables are at each site, plus a group shelter at Old Mill and Windy Point.
  • Pit latrines only—boil or bring your own potable water.
  • Quads, dirt bikes or any other form of ATV are not permitted. Boat motors are limited to 10 hp or less. No hunting is permitted around any of the campsite locations.

Rates

Shoulder Season (Easter – May Long Weekend; Labour Day – Thanksgiving): $15 per night per party.

Peak Season (May Long Weekend – Labour Day): $20 per night per party.

A party is defined as a family including immediate family members, or per vehicle.

Payment for reserved sites must be made in advance of the reserved time. Cash only—we do not accept cheques or credit cards.

Firewood is available from each campsite host for $6/bundle (large Rubbermaid bin). Cutting of firewood is not permitted.

Reservations

Waterfront Sites: 3-night minimum on regular weekends, 4-night minimum on long weekends.

Non-Waterfront Sites: 2-night minimum on regular weekends, 3-night minimum on long weekends.

7-day minimum notice required and only available during Peak Season (May Long Weekend – Labour Day). Otherwise, first come, first serve.

Call 250-714-9525 or email: reservations@nlcampsite.com

Getting There

From the South (northbound on Hwy 1):

  1. Take the Wellington / Nanaimo Lakes off-ramp exit (approx. 2 km north of Cassidy Airport) & continue to circle under the highway now heading for Nanaimo Lakes
  2. Continue west on Nanaimo River Rd. for approx. 25 km until you reach the Timberwest Main Gate (end of pavement)

From the North (southbound on Hwy 19 / Nanaimo Parkway):

  1. Turn left at Fifth St. (south of Jingle Pot Rd. near Malaspina College)
  2. Head east on Fifth St. for one block then turn right at the first light onto Wakesiah Ave.
  3. Head south for one block then turn right again onto Harewood/Nanaimo Lakes Rd.
  4. Continue SW for approx. 15 km on Nanaimo Lakes Rd., following the pavement (which turns into S. Forks Rd.) until T-junction with Nanaimo River Rd.
  5. Turn right and head west on Nanaimo River Rd. until you reach the Timberwest Main Gate (end of pavement)—approx. 15 km.

Proceed through the Main Gate and continue for approx. 4 km. Turn left at the Nanaimo Lakes Campsite sign and continue to the three Riverside campsites. Cross the bridge over the Nanaimo River and turn left at the T-junction for Windy Point and Old Mill campsites.

Check in with Campsite Hosts at either Windy Point or Old Mill.

Gate Hours

  • Easter – Canada Day Weekend: 8 am – 5 pm (Sun–Thurs), 8 am – 8pm (Fri–Sat)
  • Canada Day Weekend– Labour Day: 8 am – 8 pm
  • Labour Day – Thanksgiving Weekend: 8 am – 5 pm (Fridays: 8 am – 8pm)
