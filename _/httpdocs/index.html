Situated approximately 35 km southwest of Nanaimo, BC, the Nanaimo Lakes Campsite is located on the shores of First Lake. Thank you for your interest in our campsites. We look forward to seeing you this summer! Visitors can enjoy 75 rustic campsites at three sites: Old Mill, Windy Point and Riverside
